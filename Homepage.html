Guiding Communities Through Crisis

Crisis Compass is a nonprofit initiative dedicated to providing clear, reliable, and accessible guidance during natural disasters and emergency situations—when timely information saves lives.

Get Involved How It Works

What Crisis Compass Provides

Practical tools designed for real-world emergency response.

AI-Guided Emergency Plans

An AI system uses neural pathfinding techniques to generate personalized evacuation and safety plans based on user-provided information such as age and frequently visited locations.

Lifesaving Information Access

Clear summaries of critical safety instructions, designed to remain understandable even when communication systems are limited or unstable.

Preparedness for Underserved Regions

A focus on areas where emergency response infrastructure and accessible guidance are often lacking, particularly in developing regions.

Our Mission

Crisis Compass was founded to address a critical gap in disaster response: the lack of accessible, personalized guidance during emergencies. Our goal is to empower individuals and communities with direction, clarity, and trust when it matters most.

Core Principles

  • Public safety over profit
  • Clarity under pressure
  • Ethical and transparent AI use
  • Accessibility for all communities

Educational Resources

Preparedness materials designed for individuals, families, and communities.

Risk Awareness Tools

Identify potential hazards and plan safer evacuation strategies in advance.
